About Me

My name is Martin Kaminski, a 23-year-old with a rather intriguing journey so far. Originally from Poland, I uprooted to the UK at the age of 13, not knowing a word of English and with no choice but to learn a new language and adapt to a different culture. This was my first ever personal challenge which I handled quite well.

Before delving into my current pursuits, I earned a bachelor's degree in Sports & Exercise Science, a testament to my diverse interests. My career kicked off as a Technology Recruitment consultant, an experience that opened my eyes to the world of technology. After a year, I found myself increasingly drawn to the tech field, prompting me to embark on a new direction. The past few months have been an exhilarating ride- I successfully completed a 3-month coding bootcamp with JustIT and have been self-studying as well as working on my own personal projects.
